What I've built.

01 / 02
Manufacturing Finance and Workforce Reporting Dashboard

End-to-end Excel reporting model and interactive Streamlit dashboard simulating a real manufacturing finance environment, covering budget vs. actual variance, workforce KPIs, and external benchmarking against live BLS data.

  • 13-tab, 6,000+ formula workbook across 10 cost centers and 12 months using a dimensional model structure
  • 18-check reconciliation tab with automated PASS/FAIL rollup using COUNTIF and cross-tab referential integrity logic
  • Workforce KPIs including turnover rate, OT rate, absence rate, and headcount gap benchmarked against real BLS CES data
  • Streamlit and Plotly dashboard with 4 tabs, 8 KPI cards, variance heatmap, and sidebar filters

02 / 02
Indiana Economic and Policy Research Platform

Multi-source public data pipeline and interactive research dashboard covering all 92 Indiana counties, aggregating Census, BLS, FRED, and USDA data to surface economic indicators, manufacturing sector analysis, and workforce demographics.

  • Six public API sources: U.S. Census ACS 5-Year, BLS QCEW, FRED, STATS Indiana, Indiana DWD, and USDA ERS
  • County-level metrics including median income, poverty rate, labor force participation, unemployment, and average wages
  • Choropleth map, trend charts, county selector, and downloadable summary tables via Streamlit
  • Full data dictionary, API citations, methodology notes, and version-controlled GitHub pipeline
